# geologix

## Overview
GeoLogix Solutions revolutionises the logistics and delivery industry with its state-of-the-art GPS and blockchain technology. This pioneering company ensures timely deliveries within designated zones through an Ethereum-based dApp, a automatically rewarding drivers for geographical compliance.
